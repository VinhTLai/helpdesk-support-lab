# Ticket 01: Password Reset

## Issue
User was unable to log in to their account after forgetting their password.

## Ticket Submission
Ticket was submitted through the osTicket user portal using a test email address to simulate a real end-user request.
<img width="1024" height="768" alt="testuser1_ticket" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7f1400c4-f0a7-4d58-bf4f-9cc31e33714f" />

## Ticket Viewing and Resolving
1. Logged in to osTicket as itadmin.
2. Opened TestUser1's ticket under Tickets tab.
3. Performed troubleshooting steps.
4. Responded to TestUser1's ticket with new password.
5. Marked ticket as Resolved.
<img width="1024" height="768" alt="ticket1_response" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/466317fe-ded5-4520-b96e-8e0e9365f469" />


## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Logged in as ITAdmin.
2. Right-clicked Start and opened Computer Management.
3. Opened Users folder under Local Users and Groups.
4. Right-clicked on TestUser1 and clicked "Set Password...".
5. Reset the user's password.
6. Enabled "User must change password at next login."
<img width="1024" height="768" alt="testuser1_password_reset" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f818d88d-f1e8-49e6-993d-884fbf6af338" />


## Tools Used
- Computer Management
- Local Users and Groups

## Resolution
Password was successfully reset and user was able to log in.

## What I Learned
This reinforced the importance of identity management and secure password practices.
